## ðŸŽ¬ Customer Interview Script Template

### Opening (2 min)
"Hi [Name], thanks for your time. I'm building a product to help companies with customer support, and I'd love to understand your challenges better. This is NOT a sales call - I'm just learning. Is that okay?"

### Questions (20 min)

**Current State:**
1. What's your biggest customer support challenge?
2. How many support requests do you get per day/week?
3. What languages do you need to support?
4. What tools do you currently use?
5. How much do you spend on support per month?
6. How many people are on your support team?

**Pain Points:**
7. What keeps you up at night regarding support?
8. What would make your life easier?
9. What's the most frustrating part of support?
10. What happens when support is overwhelmed?

**Future:**
11. What would ideal support look like?
12. Would you pay for a solution? How much?
13. What would need to be true for you to try it?
14. Who else should I talk to?

### Closing (3 min)
"Thank you so much! Based on what you've shared, [summarize their pain]. Would you be open to seeing a demo of what we're building? No pressure - just want to show you what we're working on."
